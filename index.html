About Department

    
    The Department of Information Technology was started in the year 2001 with the intake of 60 students. From year 2019-20 department has offering B. Tech. in 
 Computer Science & Information Technology.  The department has got two years of Accreditation by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) in the academic year 2013-14.

    Our training & placement department works hard to make students liable to face interviews. Due to this efforts last three year’s placement is more than 90%.
 Highest package offered is 15 Lakh per annum.br Department received Rs. 1 Lakh fund from Unnat Bharat Abhiyan for research project proposal 
“Villagers Grievance Redressal: E- Complaint Management System". Also received Rs. 3.10 Lakh from Rajiv Gandhi Science & Technology Commission for project “Company Connect”.
